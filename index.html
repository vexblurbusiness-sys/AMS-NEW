FAQs

Find answers to common questions about home loans, refinancing, overseas income lending and the loan application process in Australia.

How do I know how much I can borrow?

Your borrowing capacity depends on income, expenses, liabilities, deposit, property purpose and lender assessment.

Can I get a home loan in Australia with overseas income?

Some lenders may consider overseas income, but requirements vary by lender, currency, status, employment type and documentation.

Can temporary visa holders apply?

Some temporary visa holders may be eligible depending on visa type, income, deposit, property purpose and lender policy.

Are the interest rates guaranteed?

No. Rates are indicative and subject to change. Your actual rate depends on lender assessment and your full financial situation.
