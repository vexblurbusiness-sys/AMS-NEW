Full-Doc and Alternative-Doc Pathways

Document pathways depend on lender policy, trading history and the strength of your business income evidence.

Full-docUsually uses tax returns, notices of assessment and full business financials to verify income.
Alternative-docMay use BAS, accountant declarations, bank statements or other records where lender policy allows.

Income Items to Discuss

  • Business trading history and industry
  • Taxable income and add-backs
  • Director wages, dividends or trust distributions
  • Business debts, leases and recurring costs
  • Cash flow seasonality and recent revenue changes
