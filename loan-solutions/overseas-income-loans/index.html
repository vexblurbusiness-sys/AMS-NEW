Overseas income is not one category. Currency, country, employment type and residency status all change the lender conversation.

Common Questions We Review

  • Which currency are you paid in?
  • Are you an Australian citizen, resident, temporary visa holder or foreign buyer?
  • Is the income salary, commission, bonus or business income?
  • Can documents be translated and verified?
  • What deposit and loan-to-value ratio are you targeting?
