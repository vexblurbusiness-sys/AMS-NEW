Glossary and Document Basics

Understand common lending terms before you compare options or speak with a broker.

LVRLoan-to-value ratio: the loan amount compared with the property value.
LMILenders Mortgage Insurance: may apply when borrowing above certain LVR thresholds.
Pre-approvalAn early lender assessment that still depends on conditions and property checks.
Comparison rateA rate example that includes certain fees and charges for a specific loan scenario.
