Specialist Scenario FAQs

Questions for overseas income and self-employed applicants.

Lenders may assess currency, country, employer type, visa status, tax records, translations and bank credits differently. Not every lender accepts the same income evidence.
Depending on the lender and pathway, documents may include personal and business tax returns, financial statements, BAS, accountant letters, bank statements and company or trust records.
Alternative-doc pathways may use documents other than full tax returns where lender policy allows. They are not suitable for every borrower and usually require a careful policy review.
