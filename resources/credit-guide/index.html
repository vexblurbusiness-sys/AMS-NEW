Broker role

How a Broker Helps

A mortgage broker can help compare lender policies, loan structures and product options, then assist with the application process where a suitable pathway is identified.

Before making a decision, you should consider whether a loan is suitable for your circumstances and ask for clarification on fees, commissions, lender criteria and ongoing obligations.

Before You Send Personal Documents

Protect your identity and financial information. Ask which secure channel should be used before sending sensitive documents.

  • Confirm who is requesting the document and why it is needed.
  • Do not send passwords, banking logins or verification codes.
  • Keep copies of important communication and complaint records.
  • If you are under financial stress, contact your lender early and seek independent support where appropriate.
